## Game Rules:

### Step 1: Select Your Method
Choose a fishing style:
- **Peaceful (Peaceful)** ‚Äî Catching non-predatory species.
- **Predatory (Predatory)** ‚Äî Hunting for carnivorous fish.

### Step 2: Cast the Line
Press key `D` to throw the bait into water.

### Step 3: Wait for Bite
The game waits briefly (~10 seconds) until there‚Äôs a bite from a fish.

### Step 4: Reel In
When you feel a bite, press key `C` to bring the fish closer to the shore.

### Step 5: Decide What To Do With Caught Fish
After successfully landing a fish, decide whether to:
- **Sell** it by pressing `S`. Earn coins based on the type of fish.
- **Store** it in your inventory by pressing `T`.

### Step 6: Repeat Steps
Continue fishing as much as you'd like!
